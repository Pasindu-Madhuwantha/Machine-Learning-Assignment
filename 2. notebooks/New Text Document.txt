Bayesian Ridge Regression: 

Bayesian Ridge Regression offers a robust approach for stock market prediction. By incorporating Bayesian principles, it provides probabilistic estimates of model parameters, accommodating uncertainty inherent in financial data. Its regularization term helps prevent overfitting, crucial for noisy financial datasets. This method's flexibility allows it to capture complex relationships in stock market dynamics, while also adapting to changes in data over time.
